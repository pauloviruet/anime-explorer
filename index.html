Anime Explorer

How to Use Anime Explorer

  • Search: Start typing the name of an anime (at least 3 characters) in the search bar to get results.
  • Random Must-Watch: Click this button to see a random list of recommended anime titles.
  • Toggle Theme: Switch between light and dark modes for a better viewing experience.
  • Toggle Link Source: Choose between AnimeKai and HiAnimeZ for external anime links.
  • Click Anime Titles: Clicking on a title will prompt you to open the anime page on the selected website in a new tab.
  • Click on Cards: Click on an anime card to see more details (title and description).
